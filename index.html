Background

The Academy integrates five progressive modules each with its distinct learning objectives, live and asynchronous components:

  • Organizational & Workforce Transformation with AI

Instruction & Curriculum

The integrated curriculum is informed by MIT frameworks, cutting-edge research, and industry best practices, preparing organizations for responsible and effective AI adoption, particularly in healthcare and other high-stakes environments.

The program is led by Dr. Luis Barros, Lecturer at the MIT Sloan School of Management and an experienced entrepreneur, investor, and advisor with extensive technology and healthcare industry experience.

All live instruction is delivered by LBV's Technology Academy instructors.

Through LBV's applied instruction, participants translate MIT xPRO concepts into healthcare-specific clinical, operational, and product-innovation scenarios, building practical, job-ready AI capabilities alongside recognized credentialed learning.

Who Should Attend

This course is designed for healthcare leaders, clinicians, and administrators across providers, payors, and manufacturers—including biotechnology, medical devices and diagnostics, medical imaging, and digital health—as well as product managers, innovation leaders, data teams, and AI practitioners involved in designing, deploying, operating, governing, or evaluating AI systems. It is also well suited for operations, strategy, workforce, compliance, risk, policy, and governance professionals responsible for ethical and compliant AI deployment.

Why This Course Matters

Most AI failures stem not from technical shortcomings, but from misaligned expectations and misunderstanding of AI capabilities and limits. This course equips participants to evaluate AI systems realistically and responsibly, emphasizing how data quality, system design, and governance shape performance, trust, and outcomes. Participants gain insight into how AI reshapes workflows, roles, and decision authority, enabling AI adoption that is effective, trustworthy, and fit for purpose.

Process & Eligibility

Express Program Eligibility

  • Businesses with ≤100 MA employees:
    • Up to $300/hour of training
    • Up to $3,000/person per course
    • Up to $15,000/year per employer
    • 100% reimbursement for businesses with ≤50 employees
    • 50% reimbursement for businesses with 51-100 employees
  • Businesses with >100 employees: Eligible for the General Program (different reimbursement structure - contact for details)

Note: All training must be completed within the program year. Contact Commonwealth Corporation or LBV for specific eligibility questions and application assistance.
